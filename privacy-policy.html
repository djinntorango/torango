Privacy Policy

SECTION 1 - INFORMATION COLLECTION AND USAGE

When you explore our website, we automatically gather certain details about your device, such as your web browser, IP address, and the cookies stored on your device. As you navigate through the site, we track your interactions, including the web pages visited and products viewed. We refer to this collected data as “Device Information.”

We utilize various technologies for collecting Device Information, including cookies, log files, and web beacons. Cookies are data files that contain an anonymous unique identifier, while log files track actions on the site, capturing information like IP addresses and browser types.

When you make a purchase or attempt to do so, we may collect your Order Information, including your name, billing address, shipping address, payment details, email address, and phone number.

The term "Personal Information" in this Privacy Policy refers to both Device Information and Order Information.

We use Order Information to fulfill orders, process payments, arrange shipping, and communicate with you. Additionally, we may use this information for order screening and to provide you with relevant product or service information based on your preferences.

Device Information helps us identify potential risks or fraud and enhance the overall user experience on our site. We may also employ this data for analytics, evaluating customer interactions, and assessing the effectiveness of marketing campaigns.

We may send you emails about our products, store, and updates, and share your Personal Information with third parties to facilitate these services.

We may disclose your Personal Information to comply with legal obligations, respond to lawful requests, or protect our rights.

DO NOT TRACK: Please note that our data collection practices do not change in response to Do Not Track signals from your browser.

YOUR RIGHTS: If you are a European resident, you have the right to access, correct, update, or delete your personal information. To exercise these rights or for inquiries, please contact us at djinn@torango.io.

For European residents, note that we process information to fulfill contracts and pursue legitimate business interests. Your data may be transferred outside of Europe, including to Canada and the United States.

DATA RETENTION: We retain Order Information for our records unless you request deletion.

CHANGES: We may update this privacy policy periodically for operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. Please review this policy for any changes.

AGE LIMITATIONS: Our site is not intended for individuals under the age of 18.

SECTION 2 - CONSENT

When you provide personal information for transactions, we imply your consent for collection and use. We will seek direct consent for secondary purposes, such as marketing.

You can withdraw your consent at any time by contacting us at djinn@torango.io.

SECTION 3 - DISCLOSURE

We may disclose your information if required by law or if you violate our Terms of Service.

SECTION 4 - THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

Third-party providers only collect, use, and disclose information necessary for the services they provide. Check their privacy policies for how they handle personal information.

When using third-party services, your information may be subject to the laws of different jurisdictions.

When leaving our site, you are no longer governed by this Privacy Policy.

SECTION 5 - SECURITY

We take reasonable precautions to protect your personal information using industry best practices and comply with PCI-DSS requirements.

SECTION 6 - COOKIES

We use various cookies, including sessional and persistent ones. You can opt-out of cookies through your browser settings.

For a detailed list of cookies, please refer to our policy.

SECTION 7 - AGE OF CONSENT

By using our site, you confirm you are at least the age of majority in your state or province, or have given consent for your minor dependents to use our site.

SECTION 8 - CHANGES TO THIS PRIVACY POLICY

We reserve the right to modify this privacy policy. Material changes will be notified to you. If our store is acquired or merged, your information may be transferred to new owners.

QUESTIONS AND CONTACT INFORMATION

For access, correction, deletion of personal information, complaints, or more information, contact our Privacy Compliance Officer at djinn@torango.io.
